What's reflected here — every locked decision

A quick map from each call you made to where it shows up, so you can confirm at a glance before the build spec.

Nav placement — option (b)
Raised "Plan" button
The three browse tabs stay intact; Plan is a raised, accent-gradient create button in the bar, present on every screen. Reads as "make something," not a fourth destination — the locked three-section shape is preserved.
Recommendations — hybrid-lean-A
Whole days, every stop swappable
Frame 2 hands over complete days (the day-shape pills), each fillable from current happenings or evergreen. No empty canvas. Stays batch-AI-safe via curated, seasonally-refreshed day skeletons.
The five questions
When · Time-of-day · Who · Vibe · Where
All on one simple screen. Time-of-day is multi-select horizontal buttons; vibe is a curated 4 from your 10 occasion tags; "where" doubles as the geographic-clustering anchor so the day doesn't zig-zag.
Saved things in the flow
Badged inside every swap
Frame 3 — your saved spots float to the top of each block's alternates with a gold "♥ Saved" badge. Plus a "start from my saved" hook on the setup screen.
Where plans live
Saved › Days (drawer is a shortcut)
The top-right "My plans" drawer (Frame 4) is a fast shortcut; the canonical home is the Saved page, gaining a Things / Days split. One source of truth.
One Perfect SB Day → promoted
"Make My Day" flagship
The old card becomes the default express skeleton at the top of Plan. Same seed-a-day mechanic, new front door. (Alternates if you'd rather: "Make me a day" / "Daymaker pick".)
Shared view — copyable
"Save this plan" included
Frame 5 stays strictly view-only (🔒), but offers the renamed "Save this plan" copy-to-own — the recipient-becomes-a-user growth loop, riding the existing share-link infra.
Scope
Single day only
A day-planner, not a multi-day trip planner — confirmed. Everything above fits inside the no-accounts, localStorage + magic-link world already built.
Detail access from every card
Three contexts, one clean rule
Every "things" card reaches its detail screen without added density: spine cards open on tap (the header says so); selection cards carry a small ⓘ so a tap still picks while ⓘ previews; shared cards show "Details ›". Plans in the drawer open the plan, as expected.
Before I write the build spec — a few small calls

Worth your eye on the mockup

  • The raised Plan button sits in cell 3 of a four-cell bar (Explore · Saved · Plan · Discover). It's slightly right of dead-center by geometry, but the raised treatment makes it the focal point. If you'd prefer it visually dead-center, the alternative is a flat terracotta-filled Plan cell instead of a raised circle — say the word.
  • "Make My Day" — is the brand-verb wink right, or do you want the plainer "Make me a day"? Easy swap.
  • Day-shape names (Classic Coastal · Funk Zone & Wine · Arts & State St.) are placeholders — the real seasonal set is a content decision we'll lock when building the skeletons.
  • Saved-stop badge on the spine — in Frame 2 I kept the spine clean and surfaced "♥ Saved" only in the swap sheet. If you want a saved stop to wear the badge on the spine itself too, I can add it.
