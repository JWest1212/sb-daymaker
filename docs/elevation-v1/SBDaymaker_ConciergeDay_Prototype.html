How to drive it: start with "Build my day," tap an answer to each question, watch it solve, then on the itinerary try Swap on any stop, Redo to re-solve, and Share day to see the permalink. "Clear" or the ✕ starts over.

What the prototype is demonstrating
  • Expanded inputs. Who, start point, transport, budget, and meals reshape the result (family plus toddler adds nap pacing; walking keeps it in one zone).
  • A solved spine. Timed stops with transitions that name the parking reality, and a lunch stop inserted at mealtime, in-cluster and in-budget.
  • Editable, not locked. Every stop swaps to the next-best open option, Redo re-solves, and the go-bar is Share plus Clear (the plan is ephemeral, no Save).
  • Shareable. A no-login /p/… permalink with a day-card preview. No account, no stored contact info.
