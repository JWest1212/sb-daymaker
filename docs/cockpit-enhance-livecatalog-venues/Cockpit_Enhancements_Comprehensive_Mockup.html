Cockpit enhancements: everything surfaced, in one view

Two surfaces, every change we've found. Anything with a real interface is rendered in place. Behavioral, logic, and hygiene fixes (which have no pixels) are pinned as annotations on the surface they touch. Still-open calls carry a dashed OPEN pin for you to settle during review. Each section ends with a change-index checklist. Nothing here is built yet, and the Venues tab itself is still a Phase-2 deploy.

Rendered = the change drawn where it lives. Annotation callout = a fix with no visible surface, described in context. Sheets are shown expanded inline (not as pop-over modals) so you can review everything in one scroll.
